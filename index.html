Why I Like Macaroni Pie and Baked Chicken





  1. The Macaroni pie is creamy

  2. The cheese on top is perfectly crisp but melted

  3. The chicken is tender from baking

  4. The chicken isn't too greasy because it was baked


My Recipe



Macaroni Pie


  1. Boil your preferred noodles on the stove with a little oil.

    2. Whilst it boils, prepare your seasoning.
  2. To a blender, add fresh onion, garlic, bell pepper, thyme, salt, paprika, cayenne, your kitchen, your house, your car, and any other seasonings youd like to add. I like adding my whole property because it adds a nice tang.

  3. once the noodles are done, strain and add the seasoning.

  4. to the noodles, add mayo, ketchup, mustard and other preferred powdered seasonings.

  5. Finally, put the noodles in a pan and put them in the oven at 10,000 degrees kelvin to bake for two years.


Baked chicken


  1. season the chicken with preferred seasnings and put in the oven with the macoroni pie.
